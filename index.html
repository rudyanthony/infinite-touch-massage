FAQ

Before you book.

What is the difference between massage and an assessment session?

Massage is standard bodywork. The assessment session is a more focused 90-minute visit for recurring tension, posture, and desk-related issues.

What is Chop Shop Follow-Up?

It is a shorter 30–45 minute follow-up for returning clients who want focused work between full sessions.

Do you offer chair massage for events?

Yes. Chair massage is available for offices, wellness events, private gatherings, and pop-ups. Pricing starts at $100 per hour.

Do you travel to clients?

Yes. Mobile massage is available in Miami and nearby service areas.
