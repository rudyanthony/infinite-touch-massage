About

Bodywork that feels personal, grounded, and intentional.

Infinite Touch Massage is a mobile massage practice built for people who want quality care without the rush, noise, or impersonality that often comes with larger platforms. Licensed since 2012, Rudy brings a thoughtful blend of professionalism, presence, and hands-on experience to every session.

  • Licensed and insured mobile massage therapist serving Miami and surrounding South Florida areas.

Practice philosophy

Healing should feel simple, not performative.

That means a calm presence, attentive bodywork, and sessions that respond to real tension, real stress, and real life. The goal is not just a massage that feels good in the moment, but care that helps you feel more at home in your body afterward.

FAQ

Before you book.

A few clear answers to help first-time clients know what to expect from a mobile massage session.

How do I book a session?

You can call, text, or email to request a session. At this stage, booking stays direct and personal rather than automated.

Do you travel to clients?

Yes. Infinite Touch Massage is a mobile massage service serving Miami and nearby South Florida neighborhoods in the comfort of your home or approved location.

What happens before the massage begins?

Each session starts with a short intake and conversation about your goals, pressure preferences, and areas of focus so the treatment can be tailored to you.

What style of massage do you offer?

Sessions may include Swedish massage, Shiatsu-inspired bodywork, and sports massage principles, blended according to what will serve you best.

Do you offer memberships and referrals?

Yes. Monthly Rhythm offers member pricing for recurring care, and the referral program rewards you when a new referred client completes a session.
